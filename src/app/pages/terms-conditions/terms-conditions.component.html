Terms and Conditions

Hey there! Welcome to Animal Adoption Ltd.'s Symbolic Animal Adoption Program. Here, we've laid out the rules and guidelines for adopting animals via our website.

1. Introduction

Just so you know, adopting an animal on our site is symbolic. It means you don't actually get to take home or look after a real animal.

2. Symbolic Adoption

When you symbolically adopt, you're throwing your support behind our animal conservation, care, and education initiatives.

3. Adoption Contribution

Your adoption contribution is actually a donation. Once made, it's not refundable. We use these funds to keep doing great work in animal care and conservation.

4. Adoption Certificate

With each symbolic adoption, you'll get a digital certificate. It's a nice keepsake but remember, it's just symbolic and doesn't mean you legally own or have rights over the animal.

5. Personal Information

Your privacy matters to us. We'll only use the personal info you provide during the adoption process to process the adoption itself. We promise not to hand it out to anyone else without your OK.

6. Limitation of Liability

Just a heads up, Animal Adoption Ltd. won't be on the hook for any direct or indirect issues, or any kind of damage that might come up from adopting with us.

7. Changes to Terms and Conditions

Occasionally, we might tweak these terms and conditions. You can always find the latest version on our website.

8. Contact Us

Got questions about all this? Feel free to reach out at contact@virtualwildlife.org.
